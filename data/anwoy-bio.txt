Anwoy Chatterjee is a second-year PhD student at IIT Delhi, supervised by Prof. Tanmoy Chakraborty. His research focuses on making LLMs more robust, interpretable, and adaptable to mixed-context learning scenarios. Anwoy is a Google PhD Fellow (2024) in Natural Language Processing. Before starting his PhD, Anwoy completed his BTech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT (BHU) Varanasi.
